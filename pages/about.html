Our teaching pedagogy was developed by academic experts passionate about children.


Founded in response to the increasing stresses of society on grades, 5 professors in art-driven early childhood development adopted a gamification model with an emphasis on learning by art. The Crayon Box is thus far the only institute to have an art-focused gamification learning environment. Cited along Swan Lake, the institute has an urban campus with access to amazing natural resources. We often bring our children outdoors and encourage creative expression.

Oh and we also believe that the learning environment is a pivotal factor. It's why we spared no expense to ensure our campus is carefully, and beautifully designed to ensure the best for your children
